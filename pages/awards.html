NSERC - Undergraduate Student Research Award (2017)

A scholarship issued by the government valued $4500, to a student participating in research in the field of Science or Engineering. I received this award during my first co-op term working as a research student for the University of Waterloo on Machine Learning and Database optimization.

UWaterloo President’s Scholarship of Distinction (2016)

An admission entrace scholarship valued $2000 issued to full-time first-year degree studies at Waterloo who are beginning post-secondary studies for the first time with an admission average of 95%+.

Governer General Academic Medal (2016)

This medal is awarded to the student with the highest grade average of the term. Only one individual per school is given this award. I received this medal in 2016 with a 98% cumulative average.
