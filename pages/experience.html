University of Waterloo/MyEffect Inc. - Full Stack Developer

I am currently on my first co-op internship as a full stack web developer at MyEffect Inc, which is a service platform that allows users keep track of their social impacts to promote leadership and community involvement. The platform is built using many languages and tools: AngularJS, Bootstrap, and Sass are the major front-end components and SQL, Sequelize, Redis, and Node are the major server back-end components.

In addition, MyEffect is also partnering with a professor at the University of Waterloo on a project that focuses on implementing a self-adaptive platform in optimizing database efficiency. The desired result would be that based on the user's activities, the database will adjust itself to optimize its performance.

Being a full-stack developer, I am responsible for a variety of tasks including UI design, implementing new features, server authentication, bug reporting and QA. Since the company is a start-up, each employee's contribution is much more significant and noticeable. In addition, being an undergraduate research student for the school, I also have other tasks including report writing, as well as conducting research on web optimization, which contributes to the on-going research project the university is working on.
